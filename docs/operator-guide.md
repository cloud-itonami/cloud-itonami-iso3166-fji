# Operator guide — FJI

Portal: no bespoke government-built e-procurement self-service portal
was found reachable this session -- the Fiji Procurement Office's own
site (a division of the Ministry of Economy) instead links a
third-party white-label e-tendering platform, `portal.tenderlink.com/
economyfiji`, regulated by the Government Tender Board under the
Procurement Regulations 2010. Entity: Registrar of Companies Office
(Ministry of Justice, Companies Act 2015) registration + FRCS (Fiji
Revenue and Customs Service) TIN record. Simplified-procurement
engagements (reg 29, FJD 50,000-and-under track) also clear the
Regulations' own value-threshold eligibility gate before a filing can
proceed. Human-gated filing only.
