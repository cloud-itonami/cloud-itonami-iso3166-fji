# Business model — FJI

Independent public-sector market-entry compliance for the Republic of
Fiji.

- Fiji Procurement Office (a division of the Ministry of Economy)
  public procurement -- Procurement Regulations 2010, made under the
  Finance Management Act 2004: reg 4/5 establish the Office and its
  functions, regs 9-20 establish and govern the Government Tender
  Board, reg 29/reg 30 fix a FJD 50,000/50,001 procurement-method
  value threshold (see `src/marketentry/facts.cljc`); no bespoke
  government-built e-procurement self-service portal was found reachable
  this session -- the Fiji Procurement Office's own site instead links a
  third-party white-label e-tendering platform, `portal.tenderlink.com/
  economyfiji`
- Registrar of Companies Office (Ministry of Justice) business/company
  registration under the Companies Act 2015 / Companies Regulation 2015,
  with a live digital registry at `mobile.digital.gov.fj`
- Fiji Revenue and Customs Service (FRCS) TIN (Tax Identification
  Number) registration via TPOS (Taxpayer Online System) -- compulsory
  for all forms of business per FRCS's own registration-information page
- Procurement Regulations 2010 reg 29/reg 30 "simplified-procurement"
  value-threshold gate -- bars a filing that declares itself on the
  simplified (FJD 50,000-and-under) track when its own declared contract
  value actually requires Government Tender Board review
